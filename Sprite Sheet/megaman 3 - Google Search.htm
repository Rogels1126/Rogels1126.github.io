      How do you use topspin in Mega Man 3?
      Mega Man 3
      Top Spin is obtained by defeating Top Man. To activate it, Mega Man must be up in the air and press the attack button to spin. Top Spin uses one weapon energy unit when it hits an enemy. Using it without hitting anything will not use up energy.

      What is Needle man weak to?
      Needle Man is one of the four bosses fought in the Wily Castle. His actions are the same from Mega Man 3. His weakness is the Top Spin due to the Gemini Laser not being in this game. Air Shooter is also a good weapon to use against him here.
